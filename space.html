✅ Space Facts

🌍 Earth is not a perfect sphere

It bulges at the equator due to its rotation.
Source: NASA

🌑 The Moon is slowly moving away from Earth

It drifts about 3.8 cm away every year.
Source: NASA

☄ Space is not completely empty

It contains gas, dust, and radiation.
Source: ESA

🌞 The Sun makes up 99.8% of the Solar System's mass

Most of the system's gravity comes from the Sun.
Source: NASA Solar System Exploration

🪐 Saturn is the least dense planet

It would float in water if you could find a big enough tub!
Source: NASA

❌ Space Myths

🚫 You explode in space without a suit

✅ False. You won’t explode, but you'll lose consciousness in ~15 seconds.
Source: NASA

🚫 The Great Wall of China is visible from space

✅ False. It’s hard to see with the naked eye.
Source: Scientific American

🚫 The Moon has a dark side

✅ False. Both sides receive sunlight; one is just not visible from Earth.
Source: NASA

🚫 Black holes suck everything in

✅ False. Only objects within a certain distance (event horizon) get pulled.
Source: ESA

🚫 The Sun is yellow

✅ False. It's actually white — Earth's atmosphere makes it appear yellow.
Source: NASA
