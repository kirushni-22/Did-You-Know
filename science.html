✅ Science Facts

🌍 Earth is not a perfect sphere

It bulges at the equator and flattens at the poles.
Source: NASA

☀ The Sun is a star

It is a massive burning sphere of gas producing light and heat.
Source: NASA

💧 Water expands when it freezes

This is why ice floats on water.
Source: Science.org

🧠 The brain works at night

It stays active even during sleep.
Source: WHO

🌡 Human body has 37°C average temperature

That's approximately 98.6°F.
Source: WHO

❌ Science Myths

🚫 Humans use only 10% of their brains

This is a myth. We use almost all parts of our brain.
Source: WHO

🚫 Lightning never strikes the same place twice

It does! Tall buildings get hit repeatedly.
Source: NWS

🚫 Vaccines cause autism

Completely false. Repeated studies prove vaccines are safe.
Source: WHO

🚫 The Great Wall of China is visible from space

Not without aid. It's not visible to the naked eye from space.
Source: NASA

🚫 Sugar causes hyperactivity in children

No scientific link has been proven.
Source: Healthline
