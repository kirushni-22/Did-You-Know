✅ Historical Truths

📜 The Great Wall of China is not visible from the Moon

NASA confirmed it's not visible without aid from space.
Source: NASA

⚔ Napoleon Bonaparte was of average height

He was 5’6”, normal for his era. British propaganda made him seem short.
Source: Britannica

🚀 Hitler didn’t invent the Autobahn

The highway project started in the 1920s before his rule.
Source: History.com

⚖ The Salem witches weren’t burned

They were hanged, not burned. Burning witches was more common in Europe.
Source: Smithsonian

🌍 Vikings didn’t wear horned helmets

Horned helmets were created for opera costumes in the 1800s.
Source: History Channel

❌ Historical Myths

🚫 Cleopatra was Egyptian

✅ False. She was Greek (descended from Macedonian rulers).
Source: Britannica

🚫 Einstein failed math

✅ False. He mastered calculus at age 15.
Source: Biography.com

🚫 Columbus discovered America

✅ False. Native peoples lived there for centuries, and Vikings arrived before him.
Source: History Channel

🚫 Marie Antoinette said "Let them eat cake"

✅ False. No historical record proves this. It was propaganda.
Source: Smithsonian

🚫 The pyramids were built by slaves

✅ False. Archaeologists found tombs of paid workers near the pyramids.
Source: National Geographic
