Backed by WHO and global research.

❌ Health Myths

🚫 Cold weather causes colds

✅ False. Viruses cause colds, not temperature.
Source: WHO

🚫 You need 8 glasses of water every day

✅ False. Needs vary by person and food intake.
Source: Mayo Clinic

🚫 Cracking knuckles causes arthritis

✅ False. No evidence supports this claim.
Source: Harvard Health

🚫 Natural = Always Safe

✅ False. Many natural substances can be harmful (like arsenic).
Source: WebMD

🚫 Sugar makes kids hyperactive

✅ False. Studies show no consistent link.
Source: National Institutes of Health
