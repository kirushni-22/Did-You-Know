✅ Technology Facts

🌍 The internet is not the same as the World Wide Web

The internet is the network; the web is a service on it.
Source: W3C

🤖 AI learns from data, not magic

AI improves through data training — not pre-written rules.
Source: MIT Technology Review

🛰 GPS works through satellites, not the internet

GPS uses satellite signals, so it works even offline.
Source: NASA

📱 5G is significantly faster and lower latency than 4G

5G enables real-time communication for IoT and vehicles.
Source: IEEE

💾 Cloud storage is not in the clouds — it's physical

Data is stored on secure servers in data centers.
Source: Google Cloud Docs

❌ Technology Myths

🚫 More megapixels = better camera

✅ False. Sensor quality and lens matter more.
Source: CNET

🚫 Macs don’t get viruses

✅ False. Macs can get malware and need protection too.
Source: Norton

🚫 Charging phone overnight ruins battery

✅ False. Modern phones manage charging automatically.
Source: Apple

🚫 Incognito mode makes you invisible

✅ False. It only hides your browsing history locally.
Source: Google Help

🚫 More RAM always makes computer faster

✅ False. Speed depends on processor, SSD, and optimization too.
Source: TechRadar
